Olympid

Memories of Olympid 2012

Sportex

The Textile Institute of Pakistan is a leading institute imparting textile education in all related fields of textile since 1994. The Textile Institute of Pakistan is striving to tap appropriate channels to contribute whole-heartedly for the development of society as a whole. To further this cause, TIP has established a Sports Society, Sportex.

The society works with an aim to reach out to different other educational institutes and exhibit its students' capabilities throughout. Students from Textile Institute of Pakistan have been exhibiting their sportsmanship at HEC tournaments and also at different interuniversity level events organized by different universities in Pakistan.

Olympid 2015 is one of the biggest sports event organized by Sportex in which students from different universities, High-schools and colleges of Karachi participate to show evidence of their sports-man spirit.
